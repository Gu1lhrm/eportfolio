Fast Fix

Click HERE to see the project

The main goal of the project was to assist users in replacing the internal battery of an Asus ROG GA502DU laptop and post it on the iFixit website. My project provided a step-by-step guide with detailed images and clear instructions on how to conduct the process. The information is presented in such a way that people with little to no experience opening and repairing their laptops can complete it.

In general, the project presented 8 steps. Each step contains instructions on how to proceed, accompanied by images visually demonstrating what the user should see at each step. To ensure high-quality photos, I used a Sony a6000 with a 0.25mm lens in Superior Auto mode. Every step I took, such as unscrewing the bottom of the computer, was documented with a close-up image, and I noted what I was doing. After the entire process was complete, I sent the photos and instructions to my partner to confirm their quality, and then proceeded to upload them to the iFixit platform.

My contributions

During the project's development, the workload was divided equally among each person in the group according to their access. For example, since the computer used during the guide was mine, I was responsible for disassembling it and noting the steps I was taking – taking photos and writing them down step by step. I was also responsible for informing my partner of my progress and, with their help in selecting the photos, uploading them to the iFixit platform.

Over the weeks, I was also responsible for developing early drafts of the instructions, while my partner focused on improving my presentation and communicating directly with the iFixit team. When we received feedback (from both peers and the iFixit team), we worked together to understand what needed to be modified to improve our project and divided the tasks equally. For example, while my partner made visual improvements to the instructions, I redid the process, taking new photos, following the feedback suggestions.

Reflections & Applications

This project helped me understand the importance of maintaining clear language when giving instructions, enabling me to communicate with people who don't have an IT background. Furthermore, since we worked in pairs, this project helped me improve my collaboration and communication skills, teaching me that expressing myself objectively is essential for fluid communication and project success. In addition, through constant feedback from various people (peers and the iFixit team), I learned not to take criticism personally and to use it constructively to improve my product. In addition to communication skills, I had the opportunity to open a computer and learn how each part works to remove only the desired component without affecting the others, capturing the process through professional-quality images that visually present this process. All of this applies not only to university projects but also to various careers where I will have to interact with people from different backgrounds and communication styles and where I would need to explain a process in an organized and clear manner.

Thinking about the professional perspective, I realize that what I learned during this process can be directly applied to various areas of professional collaboration and communication. Since I had to create a guide for people from diverse backgrounds, I had to learn to communicate technical information using clear and objective language so that non-technical people could understand what was being presented and follow the guide. Like the other project, I improved my communication skills with my partner, my colleagues, and the iFixit writing team. With the first two, I had to provide feedback so that their work could be improved, teaching me that I should use motivating language while presenting areas for improvement objectively. Regarding the feedback received, I learned that it refers to the projects that were done, not to me personally, which helped me to receive criticism professionally and improve the documents based on it. For my future career, I will keep in mind what I learned so that I can succeed both individually and in group projects, demonstrating quality and professionalism.
